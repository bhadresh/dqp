Network Engineer

Full-Time

San Francisco, CA, United States
Description
Digg is looking for people with an interest in network engineering. Come help us build and maintain the infrastructure that keeps Digg running.  You will be helping support our internal infrastructure usedby Developers, R & D and Operations as well as maintaining public infrastructure used  by millions of people a day.
Responsibilities
  • Manage physical and logical configuration of all network devices including: switches, load balancers, and routers
  • Work closely with all functional groups to maintain and build Digg's infrastructure

  • 2-4 years experience (minimum)

  • Switching/routing configuration including: Vlans, Spanning Tree, BGP, OSPF, VRRP

  • Good understanding of common ip protocols: TCP, UDP, ICMP
  • Basic UNIX knowledge
  • Able to lift at least 40 lbs
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
